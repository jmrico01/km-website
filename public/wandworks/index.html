Wand Works: Spell Simulator

A short interactive environment developed for the Wand Works event in the Cotsen Children's Library. You can cast six different spells from the Harry Potter universe, each with its own unique set of visual effects. This interactive environment runs much like a slideshow, requiring just the use of the mouse and the spacebar key. This is a consequence of its specific design for the Wand Works children's event.

Below is the gameplay footage for each of the spells in action, to provide a visual effects showcase for the project. If you can, however, I strongly recommend you try the standalone version of the game, which you can download from the Wand Works itch.io page.
