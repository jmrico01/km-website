About

Kapricorn Media is a two-man game development team. We are currently working on a 3D low-poly game based mainly on perception, but with elements of open-world exploration and survival. Its temporary title is Project Morph.

Just like cinema, literature, and music, games can be a window to very very absurd and uncomfortable worlds.
